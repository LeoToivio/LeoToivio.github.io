ASSIGNMENT

Keep working with our Suicide Game by adding two or more of the following functionalities:

  • Make a trampoline that the ball can jump on
  • Make pickups that decrease the score
  • Make a power bar that fills up by pickups and drains when player dies
  • Make pickups that makes you jump high for a given time
  • Make pickups that can make you spawn one corpse without dying
  • Make pickups with some other functionality
  • Make a door that opens when the player comes close to it
  • Create a pickup system restricts the amount of pickups in world and spawns pickup at specific locations (e.g. you have 4 spawner position, there is only 1 pickup in world at a time, when you grab the pickup, it will then spawn a new one in one of the spawner positions.
  • Make a level that can only be completed using the death mechanic
  • Make a corpse spawn from dying from firepickup
  • Make a new material
  • Make more stuff in the material
  • Make a moving platform

Criteria

  • You may also choose to do something entirely different, what I am looking for is that you touch upon the following:
  • Expand any current system with new functionality
  • Keep the code as clean as possible
  • Make effort to find a suitable and effective solution to a problem (to the best of your abilities).
  • Make use of the approach of expanding the game incrementally by combining actors with small functionality and make them work in a bigger perspective, such as parent/child actors as well as using managers. This is opposed to making a big blueprint where you have lots of different functionalities
